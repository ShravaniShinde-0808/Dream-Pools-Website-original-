What is Steam?

Steam is water vapor produced when water is heated and evaporates. It’s widely used for heating, cleaning, and in wellness spaces like steam rooms.

In health and relaxation, steam therapy opens airways, improves circulation, soothes muscles, and promotes glowing skin.

What is Saunas?

A sauna is a small heated space designed for dry or wet heat sessions, encouraging sweating to detoxify the body and improve circulation.

Regular sauna use supports cardiovascular health, reduces stress, and provides deep relaxation for both body and mind.
